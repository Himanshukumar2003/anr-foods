OUR INTRODUCTION

ANR FOOD is a corporate & Industrial canteen and cafeteria service provider where meals are designed to rejuvenate energy and fuel the workforce with positivity and satisfaction. Our aim is to serve the meals with atmost care , hygiene and health. Every individual comes to have a break from their busy work schedule to create a professional bond and we are just a medium for them.

The meals are designed as per requirement, local flavour and current trend. Out team is well trained and experienced in making your break up complete meal experience.

The focus of ANR FOOD is always on freshness, hygiene and variety, for this we keep on exposing our team with new trends to provide our clients with best of the services. From a corporate lunch to working lunch to industrial catering or event within the plants and offices our team if fully equipped and experienced to give our clients comfortable meals.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the leading Corporate & Industrial Catering provider. Quality, Quantity, hygiene & timeliness is our tool to reach our vision.

Our Quality Statement

To be the most trusted partner in corporate catering, delivering consistently high-quality, nutritious and diverse meals. From sourcing fresh, hygienic ingredients to maintain top food safety protocols backed by the FSSAI, we enhance workplace wellness.

Continuous improvement, clients feedback and rigorous quality checks with employee trainings are the driving force to deliver our client with best of the best.
