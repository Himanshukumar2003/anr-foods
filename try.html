FOUNDER (MR. AJAY RAJ)

Late Shri Mr. Ajay Raj (Founder), a mechanical engineer turned entrepreneur, was the visionary behind ANR FOOD. In 2001, he founded the company with a passion for food and a mission to build a strong corporate food solutions brand. He laid the foundation, management principles, and a vibrant work culture that reflects his personality. His people-first approach and dedication continue to shape our core values in industrial catering. At ANR FOOD, we uphold his legacy as our guiding force and will always do so.

CO-FOUNDER (MRS. NEETA RAJ)

Mrs. Neeta Raj (Co-Founder), oversees all the administrative work of this company. She has been one of the pillars who brings compassion, discipline and operational excellence to the business. With a strong core in customer relations, she plays an important role in team coordination and maintaining professionalism in the company. As the founder’s wife and business partner, she adds a personal touch to every aspect of our operations, ensuring that our client feels not just served – but cared for.

FOOD & BEVERAGES SPECIALIST (MR. KUMAR SAURABH SUSHIL)

Mr. Kumar Saurabh Sushil, Chief Food & Beverage Specialist, brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in the food industry. He has worked with top hotel brands like Taj, Hyatt, ITC Hotels, Radisson Blu, and more. His vast exposure and services, carrying forward the founder Mr. Ajay Raj’s vision, has helped ANR FOOD scale to new heights. He also leads the team to become India’s leading Institutional Catering Provider. He has the knowledge to combine tradition with a forward-thinking approach to food trends, safety, and consumer behavior.

CHEF (ANDAV RAJ)

Chef Andav Raj, a professional chef with a culinary degree from the Institute of Hotel Management (IHM), has worked with some of India’s top hotel brands. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur – and helped launch five restaurants in the region. He pushes culinary boundaries by blending traditional Indian tastes with modern plating and international influences. Cooking has always been his passion, and his creativity shines in designing scrumptious menus. His attention to detail and ability to innovate with new techniques reflect his deep commitment to excellence in the culinary world.
